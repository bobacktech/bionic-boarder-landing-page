Revolutionizing the Land Paddling Experience

As demonstrated in the video, traditional land paddling faces inherent limitations in generating significant acceleration. Much of the momentum created by propelling oneself with a stick paddle is lost to the ground, resulting in reduced efficiency. Without sufficient acceleration, land paddling can become overly strenuous and less enjoyable. However, by integrating an electric drivetrain powered by a single motor and controlled via a VESC speed controller, the combined forces of motorized assistance and rider input create a dynamic and exhilarating land paddling experience.

This is where Bionic Boarder transforms the game. Our innovative app seamlessly integrates with your electric land paddle board to:

  • Detect and respond to your paddle strokes
  • Deliver an instant power boost synchronized with your movements
  • Enhance momentum retention for greater efficiency
  • Achieve powerful acceleration with minimal effort

The result? A thrilling ride that blends the physical engagement of traditional land paddling with the cutting-edge benefits of electric assistance, redefining the way you experience the sport.

About the Founder

Bobby Jaffer is a seasoned software engineer with extensive experience in developing software for real-time embedded systems, data analysis, and web applications. Driven by a passion for land paddling, he understands the challenges of generating sufficient momentum with a stick paddle to create a truly exhilarating and enjoyable experience.

By using the power of an electric motor on the paddle board to seamlessly and naturally amplify the rider's momentum from paddling with a stick, Bobby has reimagined land paddling as an exciting and fun activity for most everyone.
